English Literacy Course

Unit 2 – Places in the Community

Learn common places in your community and how to describe their locations.

🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Name common places in the community
  • Use simple sentences to describe locations and directions
  • Read signs and recognize places on a map

📝 2. Grammar Focus

📌 There is / There are

Phrase Example
There is There is a bank on the corner.
There are There are two schools in my area.

✅ Activity

Complete sentences with there is or there are:

  1. ______ a park near my house.
  2. ______ three supermarkets in our town.

📖 3. Reading Practice

My community has many places. There is a park near my house. I go to the supermarket with my family. The hospital is across from the school.

✅ Questions

  1. Where is the hospital?
  2. Who goes to the supermarket?

✍️ 4. Writing Practice

Write 3 sentences about your community:

Example:
There is a mosque near my house.
There is a park next to the school.

✅ Bonus Activity

Draw a simple map and label 5 places in English.

🗣️ 5. Speaking Practice

Practice in pairs:

A: Where is the bank?
B: The bank is next to the pharmacy.
A: Is there a school near here?
B: Yes, there is a school near the park.

✅ Activity

Use pair or group conversation to practice directions.

🎧 6. Listening Practice

✅ Task

Listen to directions and point to the correct place on a map.

Example Instructions:

  1. Go to the park.
  2. Where is the supermarket?
  3. Go to the bus stop.

🗺️ 7. Fun Activity: Community Map Game

Instructions:

  • Create a simple town map with buildings
  • Use stickers, drawings, or printed pictures
  • Add street names and directions

Practice giving and following directions with the map.

📌 8. Recap & Homework

Today We Learned:

  • Names of community places
  • Grammar: There is / There are
  • How to describe and locate places

Homework:

  1. Draw your community and label 5 places
  2. Write 2 sentences about where you go each day
