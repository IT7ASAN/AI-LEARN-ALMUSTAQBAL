English Literacy Course

Unit 5 – Simple Reading and Writing

Learn to read simple words and sentences, and practice basic writing skills.

🎯 Lesson Objective

Reading and writing are fundamental skills that open doors to learning. Today we'll build confidence with simple words and sentences!

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Recognize and read simple words and short sentences
  • Write basic words and complete short sentences
  • Practice reading aloud and writing neatly

📖 3. Read Simple Sentences

Now let's put words together to make sentences. Read slowly and point to each word as you say it.

This is a cat.

I have a pen.

It is a big bag.

She has a book.

The sun is hot.

✅ Activity:

Read aloud with your partner. Take turns reading sentences.

Tip: Try changing the words to make new sentences (e.g., "This is a dog")

📢 6. Reading Aloud Practice

Reading aloud helps with pronunciation and builds confidence in speaking English.

Choose one of these to read:

  • This is my cat.
  • I have a red pen.
  • The dog is big.
  • She has a book.
  • My cup is blue.

✅ Activity:

Read your favorite word or sentence to the class. Speak clearly and loudly!

Tip: Stand up straight and take a deep breath before you start.
