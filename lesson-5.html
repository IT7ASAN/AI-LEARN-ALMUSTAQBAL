English Literacy Course

Unit 5 – Shopping and Money

Learn to talk about shopping, prices, and money in English.

🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Use basic shopping and money vocabulary
  • Ask and answer questions about prices and items
  • Read price tags and create shopping lists

📝 2. Grammar Focus

📌 Key Structures:

How much is the bread?

I want to buy some apples.

✅ Activity

Practice mini-dialogues in pairs:

A:
How much is the rice?
B:
It's five dollars.
A:
I want to buy it.

📖 3. Reading Practice

📄 Reading Paragraph

Ali goes to the supermarket. He buys milk, bread, and fruit. The milk is \$2. The bread is \$1. The fruit is \$3. He pays the cashier.

✅ Comprehension Questions

  1. What does Ali buy?
  2. How much is the fruit?
