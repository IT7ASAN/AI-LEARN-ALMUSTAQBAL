English Literacy Course

Unit 1 – My Family and Friends

Learn vocabulary for family members and friends, and practice talking about them using pronouns and possessives.

🎓 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify family members and friends using vocabulary
  • Use subject pronouns and possessives correctly
  • Read, write, and talk about your family and friends

✍️ 2. Grammar Focus

📌 Subject Pronouns:

Pronoun Example
I I am Ali.
you You are my friend.
he He is my brother.
she She is my sister.

📌 Possessive Adjectives:

Possessive Example
my This is my brother.
your Is this your book?
his His name is Ahmed.
her Her friend is nice.

✅ Activity

Complete the sentence: "This is _____ friend."

📖 3. Reading Practice

This is my family. I have one brother and one sister. My mother is kind. My father is a teacher. We live together in a house.

✅ Comprehension Questions

  1. How many brothers does he have?
  2. What is his father's job?

✍️ 4. Writing Practice

Example:

My name is Sara. I have two sisters. My mother is a nurse. My best friend is Lina.

✅ Activity

  1. Fill in the blanks with your personal information
  2. Write two short sentences about your family

🗣️ 5. Speaking Practice

Conversation Example:

A: Who is he?
B: He is my brother.
A: What is your mother's name?
B: Her name is Huda.

✅ Activity

Introduce your friend to the class using simple sentences.

🎧 6. Listening Practice

✅ Task

Listen to a short audio about a family.

Questions to Answer:

  1. Who is the speaker talking about?
  2. What is the sister's name?

🧩 7. Fun Activity: Family Tree

Instructions:

  • Use names and pictures to create your family tree
  • You can draw or use printed templates

Great for visual learning and personal connection!

📝 8. Recap & Homework

Today's Key Points:

  • Vocabulary: family and friends
  • Grammar: pronouns & possessives
  • Practice in reading, writing, and speaking

Homework:

  1. Draw your family tree and label the members in English
  2. Write 3 short sentences about your family or best friend
