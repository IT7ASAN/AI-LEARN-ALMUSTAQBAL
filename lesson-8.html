  • Talk about your dreams and future jobs
  • Use future expressions: I want to..., I will...
  • Set personal goals using simple sentences

✍️ 2. Grammar Focus

📌 Future Expressions:

I want to... → expresses a wish

I will... → expresses a plan or promise

Examples:

  • I want to be a teacher.
  • I will learn to cook.
  • I want to help people.

✅ Activity

Write your own sentences:

  1. I want to .
  2. I will .

📖 3. Reading Practice

📄 Reading Paragraph

My name is Amal. I want to be a doctor. I will study hard. I want to help sick people. This is my dream.

✅ Comprehension Questions

  1. What job does Amal want?
  2. Why does she want that job?

✍️ 4. Writing Practice

📝 Example

I want to be a driver.
I will learn the rules.
I want to help people travel.
