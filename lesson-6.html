English Literacy Course

Unit 6 – Technology and Everyday Tools

Learn to identify and talk about technology and tools in English.

🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify and name basic technology and tools used daily
  • Use simple sentences to describe what tools and devices do
  • Ask for help using or borrowing tools politely

📖 3. Reading Practice

📄 Reading Paragraph

This is my phone. I use it to call my family. I also have a laptop. I use it to study. My flashlight helps me when it is dark.

✅ Comprehension Questions

  1. What does he use the phone for?
  2. When does he use the flashlight?
