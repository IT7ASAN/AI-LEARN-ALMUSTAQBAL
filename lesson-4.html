English Literacy Course

Unit 4 – Health and the Body

Learn to identify body parts and communicate about health in English.

🎯 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify basic body parts and health-related vocabulary
  • Express common health problems using simple grammar
  • Communicate basic needs during a visit to a doctor or clinic

📝 2. Grammar Focus

📌 Using "have/has" for Health Problems:

I have a headache.

He has a stomachache.

✅ Activity

Match health issues to people or pictures:

🧒

"I have a fever"

👩

"She has a sore throat"

👨

"He has a cough"

📖 3. Reading Practice

📄 Reading Paragraph

Sara is sick today. She has a fever and a sore throat. Her mother takes her to the doctor. The doctor gives her medicine.

✅ Comprehension Questions

  1. What is Sara's problem?
  2. Who helps her?

✍️ 4. Writing Practice
