English Literacy Course

Unit 2 – Basic Words and Greetings

Learn common English greetings, polite words, and how to start simple conversations.

💬 Lesson Objective

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Use common English greetings in daily life
  • Understand and say basic words like 'yes', 'no', 'please', and 'thank you'
  • Practice short, polite conversations

👋 1. Introduction to Greetings

Greeting means saying hello to someone. Here are common greetings in English:

Greetings and When to Use Them
Greeting When to Use
Hello Any time
Hi Informal situations
Good morning Before 12:00 PM
Good afternoon 12:00 PM – 6:00 PM
Good evening After 6:00 PM

✅ Practice:

Teacher says "Hello", students reply "Hello!"

👋 2. Saying Goodbye

When we leave, we say goodbye in different ways:

Goodbye Phrases
Phrase Use
Goodbye General goodbye
Bye Informal goodbye
See you later When you'll meet again
Good night Before sleep

✅ Practice:

Say goodbye to a classmate:

You:
"Bye!"
Friend:
"See you later!"

✨ 3. Polite Words (Magic Words)

These are magic words that make us polite:

Polite Words and Examples
Word Use Example
Please "Please pass the book."
Thank you "Thank you for your help."
You're welcome "You're welcome!"
Excuse me "Excuse me, can I ask a question?"
Sorry "I'm sorry I was late."

✅ Activity:

Match the word to the situation:

Someone gives you a gift
You need to pass in front of someone
You want someone to help you

👍 4. Yes and No

These are the two most basic answers in English:

Using Yes and No
Word Use Example
Yes "Do you like apples?" – Yes 🍎
No "Are you sad?" – No 😊

✅ Practice:

Answer these questions:

"Are you happy today?"

Yes
No

"Do you like music?"

Yes
No

💬 5. Short Conversations (Dialogue Practice)

Practice these simple dialogues:

Example 1

A:
Hello!
B:
Hi!
A:
How are you?
B:
I'm fine, thank you.

Example 2

A:
Good morning!
B:
Good morning!
A:
What is your name?
B:
My name is Ali.

✅ Activity:

Practice the dialogues in pairs.
